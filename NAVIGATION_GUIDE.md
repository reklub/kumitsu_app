## The Complete Path

### Step 1: Go to Tournaments
- Click on **Tournaments** in the main menu
- You'll see a list of all tournaments

### Step 2: Select Your Tournament
- Click on the tournament where you want to add participants
- You'll see the tournament details page with the list of participating clubs

### Step 3: Click "Manage" on Your Club
- Find your club in the "Participating Clubs" list
- Click the blue **"Manage"** link next to your club name
- This takes you to the participants list for that club

### Step 4: Click "Batch Upload (CSV)"
- On the participants page, you'll see two buttons at the bottom:
  - Blue: **"Register New Participant"** (for adding one participant)
  - **Green: "Batch Upload (CSV)"** â† Click this one!
- Click the green "Batch Upload (CSV)" button

### Step 5: Upload Your CSV File
- You're now on the batch upload page
- Download the CSV template (optional) or prepare your own
- Drag & drop your CSV file into the upload area
- Click **"Upload and Import Participants"**

### Step 6: Done! âœ…
- See the success message with the count of imported participants
- You're automatically redirected back to the participants list
- Your new participants appear in the table

## Troubleshooting Navigation

**Q: I don't see "Manage" link on the club**
- A: Make sure you're on a tournament's details page
- Click directly on the tournament name (not on the "Manage Tournament" button)

**Q: I don't see the "Batch Upload (CSV)" button**
- A: You might be on the wrong page
- Make sure you're on the participants list (you should see a table of participants)
- The button should be at the bottom in green

**Q: After uploading, I don't see the participants**
- A: Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or F5)
- The participants should appear in the list above the buttons
